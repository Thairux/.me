This portfolio is designed to be a clear front door for my work. It combines selected projects, technical writing, and a warmer visual direction that feels more personal than a generic developer template.

## Goals

- Present projects in a way that is easy to scan
- Keep writing and case studies close to the work itself
- Create a visual identity that feels calm, premium, and memorable

## What changed

The latest version improves structure, dark mode support, project presentation, and the overall narrative of the site so it says more about the work and less about the template.

## Why it matters

A portfolio should do more than list links. It should help people quickly understand what I build, how I think, and what kinds of opportunities I am a good fit for.
