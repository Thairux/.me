HOSQ is a hospital queue management system focused on real-time visibility across the patient journey. It supports self-service check-in, staff stage handling, emergency priority flags, and SMS notifications so queues stay understandable for both patients and hospital teams.

## Problem

Queues in busy service environments can become hard to track, especially when multiple stages, emergency cases, and staff handoffs are involved. HOSQ is designed to make those transitions more visible and more manageable.

## Highlights

- Patient self-service check-in without requiring a full signup flow
- Real-time queue position tracking and stage updates
- Staff dashboards for role-based actions across departments
- Emergency flags that bring critical cases to the front of the line
- SMS notifications for queue updates and turn alerts

## Tech stack

The project uses a TypeScript-based frontend, Supabase for data, auth, and real-time features, and a healthcare-oriented workflow structure built for practical queue movement.
